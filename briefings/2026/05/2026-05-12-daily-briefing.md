**Summary (EN):**
Knowledge distillation for Large Language Models (LLMs) is emerging as a critical technology for deploying AI in resource-constrained environments. This technique involves training a smaller "student" model to replicate the performance of a much larger, more complex "teacher" model. By doing so, developers can significantly reduce computational overhead, power consumption, and latency while retaining high levels of accuracy. For financial institutions, distillation offers a path toward running sophisticated AI locally for high-frequency trading and real-time risk assessment without relying on cloud infrastructure. This trend is accelerating the commercialization of AI by making it more cost-effective and accessible to a broader range of industries. As model sizes continue to balloon, distillation will remain a key focus for market participants seeking efficiency.
